                                              HOW IT WORKS 

                                              If you’re a beer lover, we have something great to share. Behold the Growler Bar! A growler is glass jug that is filled and refilled with fresh, cold, draft beer. At Brown Jug, you can choose from a selection of craft brews to some well-known favorites. 

                                              Our selection rotates frequently so it’s always fresh and interesting. Check back here to see what’s currently on tap at your nearest location. Take home your growler and keep it chilled so you can enjoy your beer for 2-3 days. Now that’s convenient! Once you've finished, simply clean your growler jug and cap with hot water and bring it back to enjoy another fresh batch.
